# IdleGame

## Overview

IdleGame is a simple and engaging game that centers around incremental progress. Players can perform actions, earn
rewards, and upgrade their abilities to achieve higher milestones. The game is designed to be playable without constant
interaction, perfect for fans of idle or incremental games.

## Features

- **Incremental Progress:** Perform actions over time to gather resources.
- **Upgrades:** Unlock upgrades to maximize rewards.
- **Idle Gameplay:** Progress even while you're not actively playing.
- **Simple and Fun:** Easy to learn with an addictive gameplay loop.

## How to Play

1. Perform actions to gather resources.
2. Use resources to unlock and upgrade abilities for greater rewards.
3. Keep playing and watch your progress grow!
